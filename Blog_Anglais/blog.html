PROJECT 30 DAY-HEALTH CHALLENGE


This is this video who make me do this awesome challenge please watch it :)

During my adolescences I met a problem, the problem of taking bad habits like stoped the sport and lets smoking or just don’t care about my proper healthy. As I grew up I realize that habits were going to follow me until I decided to change. So I writing this for those who would like change their life. I made a program for you which consists of replacing a bad routine with a good one. Example: if you are an ex-smoker or just if you want to make progress with your body like gain muscle or stay in shape this challenge is made for you.


In this project we are going to do physical exercise during 30 days we’ll call that the 30 day health challenge. So, in it I purpose you a physical program what you can adjust for you. The initial program is to do 100 push-up by day, 100 abdominals, 30 traction and 100 squat without more charge then yourself, If you are an expert , you can also charging you. For the novice I let you modify the program as yours.


Personally I’ve test the program and I’ve guest some friends of mine to test it and I received only good notices. Personally I was a smoker when I starts the program I wanted to stop smoking to be in shape because I had cardiovascular problem so I replace a routine by another one. At last I stoped to smoke, gain in endurance and in muscle. I was so happy with this program that now it’s been 3 months I still practice. At first, i tell you, it was hard. My body wouldn't follow me so as my lungs but with perseverance i've successful. I've adapting the program to my body it was 10 push-up 10 abdominals and 2 tractions hahaha but trust me the evolution was really amazing. in one week i passed of 10 push-up to 25 push-up, etc.
